I am currently a 4th-year Ph.D. candidate at the [Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence](https://ai.ruc.edu.cn/en), Renmin University of China, advised by [Prof. Ji-Rong Wen](https://gsai.ruc.edu.cn/english/jrwen/) and [Prof. Zhewei Wei](https://weizhewei.com/).
In June 2022, I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the Turing Class at the [School of Information](http://info.ruc.edu.cn/Home/index.htm), Renmin University of China.

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, with a focus on **sublinear graph algorithms** and **spectral graph theory**.
A central theme of my research is the study of sublinear algorithms in the [Laplacian Paradigm](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-642-13562-0_2).
I am currently working on sublinear-time algorithms for estimating (Personalized) PageRank, for (asymmetric) Laplacian and diagonally dominant linear systems, and for local graph clustering, as well as sublinear-space streaming algorithms for spectral sparsification.

Since February 2026, I have been visiting the [National University of Singapore](https://nus.edu.sg/), working with [Prof. Yu Chen](https://sites.google.com/view/chenyu94).
During the spring of 2025, I visited the [Institute for Theoretical Computer Science (ITCS)](https://itcs.sufe.edu.cn/main.htm) at the [Shanghai University of Finance and Economics](https://english.sufe.edu.cn/), working with [Prof. Tsz Chiu Kwok](https://itcs.sufe.edu.cn/54/20/c10495a152608/page.htm).
My [Erdős number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erd%C5%91s_number) is 3.
